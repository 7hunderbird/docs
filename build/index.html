    • Introduction

    Instruqt is a gamified learning platform, that teaches you IT technologies and their related concepts through hands-on challenges.

    History

    Instruqt has been around in some form since the summer of 2016 under various names.

    HashiConf EU 2016, Amsterdam

    In the summer of 2016 Xebia co-hosted the European edition of HashiConf, where we had a prominent booth. We wanted to showcase our expertise through hands-on. It started it's life as the HashiContest

    HashiConf US 2016, Napa

    Presentation at hashiconf about gamification.

    Google DevFest 2016, Amsterdam

    KubeContest at event.

    Google Cloud Next 2017, San Francisco

    First release of instruqt
